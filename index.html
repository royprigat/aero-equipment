About Us

"Aeroequipment" LTD. Israel was established in 1980 by Mr. Eliezer Prigat, a retired airforce pilot in the Israeli Air Force. Our field of operation and expertize is the Cable and Wire Market, in which we deal with a variety of cables including Fiber Optics. Aeroquipment is a certified supplier for the Israeli Ministry of Defense.
